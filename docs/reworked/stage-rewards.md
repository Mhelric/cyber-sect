### 🔓 **The Chapter 1 Unlockable Arsenal**
*Cards added to the deck as the player progresses.*

**7. Spam Filter [Uncommon]** (Unlock: Stage 2)
* **Cost:** 1 RAM | **Effect:** 10 Dmg. Exhausts Junk; +3 Dmg per card.
* **Purpose:** Clears the board of Adware pop-ups.

**8. Boot Scan [Common]** (Unlock: Stage 3)
* **Cost:** 2 RAM | **Effect:** 5 Dmg. **If Malware: 25 Damage.**
* **Purpose:** The specialized tool needed to "Two-Tap" the Trojan in Stage 4.

**9. Backup Recovery [Uncommon]** (Unlock: Stage 4)
* **Cost:** 1 RAM | **Effect:** **Heal 10 HP**.
* **Purpose:** Reversing the damage from the Zero-Day's Turn 3 Crash.

**10. Air Gap [Rare]** (Unlock: Stage 5)
* **Cost:** 2 RAM | **Effect:** 15 Shield. +1 RAM next turn.
* **Purpose:** Master-level defense awarded for completing Chapter 1.
