# 👾 **CyberSect: Complete Campaign Progression**

This roadmap outlines the evolution of your deck from the first technical breach to the mastery of the human layer. Each chapter introduces a new **Fixed Blueprint** of 10 unique cards (20 total).

---

## 📂 **Chapter 1: The Perimeter (Encounter & Unlock Map)**

| Stage | Card Reward (Post-Battle) | Strategy/Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **The Rookie Starter Set** | Provides the 6 core tools for basic network/malware defense. |
| **2** | **Spam Filter** | Essential utility for clearing "Junk" cards from your hand. |
| **3** | **Boot Scan** | Massive specialized damage to handle high-HP Malware. |
| **4** | **Backup Recovery** | Direct healing to recover from system-wide crashes or exploits. |
| **5** | **Air Gap** | High-tier defense and RAM management for completing the chapter. |

### 📈 **System Rules Recap**
* **Deck Consistency:** You always carry **10 Unique Cards (20 Total)**.
* **Hand Management:** Draw **2 cards/turn** up to a **10-card Max Hand**.
* **Upgrade Limit:** Starter/Common (v3.0), Uncommon (v2.0), Rare (Read-Only).
