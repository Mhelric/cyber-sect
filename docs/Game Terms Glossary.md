# 📖 CyberSect: Mechanics & Keyword Glossary

* **Exhaust:** When a card is played, it is removed from your deck for the rest of the current battle. It will not reshuffle into your draw pile. It returns to your deck after the level is over.
* **Retain:** Do not discard this card (or shield) at the end of your turn. You get to keep it for the next turn.
* **Stun (or Disable):** The target loses their next turn completely.
* **Vulnerable:** The target takes **+50% damage** from all incoming attacks.
* **Weakness:** The target deals **50% less damage** on their next attack.
* **Pierce (or Decryption):** The attack completely ignores the enemy's Firewall (Shield) and deals damage directly to their HP. 
* **Taunt:** Forces the enemy to attack a specific target (like the Temporary HP from *Honeypot*) instead of your main HP.
* **Scry:** Look at the top cards of your draw pile. You can rearrange their order so you know exactly what you will draw next.
* **Reflect:** Take the damage the enemy deals to you and immediately deal a percentage of it back to them.
* **Confused:** The enemy's attack misses you and hits themselves instead.
